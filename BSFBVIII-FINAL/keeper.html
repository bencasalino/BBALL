Dynasty | Keeper League

Inaugural Season I | 2015-16


We have been kicking around the idea of having a smaller free keerper league on the side. This year we will make it happen! Our keeper league will have all the same rules as the Head to Head League. The keeper league in theory is geared towards more serious managers. Becuasue of the intended longevity of the leauge we would rather have less managers and expand in the future. Having a manager become inactive in this league could be catastrophic to the legitimacy of the league and could be dificult to replace managers.

Inaugural Season I (2015-16) Draft


The hardest part about starting this league will be about having a fair way to decide intial picks of the first draft.


Online Stream: I will personally host a livestream at somepoint in late summer where I will draw names from start to finish and post it on a draftboard with complete transparancey.


Website Random Number Generator: There are sites that specifically will give you draft numbers when paramaters are put in. I would record or trake screenshots of the process.

ESPN Default: One Hour before the draft EPSN will randomize the order. I personally would not prefer to only have one our to decide who will be on my team for multiple years.
